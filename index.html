Who are We?

Young ideas is not a specifc person. Young Ideas is a community of people who believe in young ideas: a community of people who believe that investing in the talents and intellect of young people is what can gurrantee a sustainable future. This is platform for any youth to share their work, be it a young cook who wants to share a recipe of their spin to a famous cuisine, a young programmer who wants to share their exciting app and how it was built, or even a young photographer who wants to share their thrilling wildlife photos taken on their trip to Africa. The embodiment and statue of the website revolves around the need for youths and young adults to express themselves, their ideas, their passions and their projects to a keen and engaging audience in an environment of like ambitious and audacious minds. With very few plartforms for young people to showcase their thouhts, ideas, and talents, Young IDEAS is a great place to start.

WHO is Young?

All the projects on this website are works of young people up to the age of 25 years. We help empower young people to showcase their gifts and talents to a broader audience. Whatever gift you have, whether it's that big mouth of yours that makes people laugh, or your strong interest in zoology; from science, to arts, and even music, YoungIDEAS is a great place for you to showcase your talents.

WHY US?

Get to experience a dedicated audience that is here just connect with you. Showcase your project to an audience with potential investors, partners, and fellows young ambitious dreamers there to inspire you and motivate you to do even more greater stuff in life.
